Redefining African Luxury

Natanem Wendosen's vision merges Ethiopia's ancient textile tradition with contemporary minimalism. Each collection tells a story through handwoven silks from Bahir Dar and intricate embroidery inspired by Lalibela's Coptic churches.

After transforming Addis Ababa's fashion scene in 2015, she now caters to a global audience seeking rare, handcrafted pieces. Each season features just 8-12 made-to-order designs, completed in 45 days.

"We don't create clothes - we craft wearable heirlooms."
